Display Keyword Targeting: An Effective Way to Reach Audience.

  • By Amzad Hossain
  • Keyword Targeting

Ad relevance is one of the most important things when it comes to launching a new campaign. In display advertising it is always a major concern to show your ads on news which are relevant to your product. So, targeting content is the key here.

Purple Patch, one of the leading Advertising Technology Companies in Bangladesh introduces keyword targeting and contextual targeting for the first time in the local market. With Purple Patch Keyword Targeting powered by our in-house, highly scalable, real-time crawl engine Purple Ghost ©, it's now easier than ever to target specific Bengali/English keywords to reach readers of specific content/demographics.

Purple Ghost seamlessly crawls millions of articles & web pages every day in the background, analyzes content with its artificial intelligence (Purple NLP based on Caffe deep learning) and curates the metadata in its blazing fast in-memory database for fast serving. Purple Ghost currently serves over 6,000 requests per second with an average latency of 2ms, which is almost 50x-75x faster than the average time for a human to blink an eye.

This is also a powerful brand-safety tool which can be used as an anti-targeting parameter to filter and review contextual placements and keywords to ensure your ads appear only among the content you find appropriate.

In recent days Purple Patch has done some successful campaigns for our several valuable clients by targeting different keywords like Covid-19, Winter, Beauty & Health etc. We have measured the campaign success by different matrices like Reach, CTR, Visible CTR etc. 

THIS TARGETING OPPORTUNITY OFFERS ADVERTISERS TO PLACE ADVERTISEMENT BESIDE THE ARTICLE, THAT INCLUDES THE SELECTED KEYWORDS.
